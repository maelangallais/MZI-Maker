# 🧑‍💻 Mach-Zehnder Interferometer Maker

An interactive, visual MZI simulation designed to generate custom, print-ready G-Code.

### 🚀 Features
* **Dynamic Visualization:** Adjust your settings in real-time and instantly see your Mach-Zehnder Interferometer take shape.

* **Slicing Control:** Zoom and navigate through the MZI design to preview the exact slicing steps and toolpaths before manufacturing.

* **Customizable G-Code:** Fine-tune your output by toggling purge lines, ironing, full MZI drawing, and custom comments.

* **Instant Export:** Download your tailored G-Code in one click, ready for execution.
